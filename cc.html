V-sit

This exercise involves balancing on your glutes with your legs and upper body raised, forming a 'V' shape. Maintaining this position requires significant core strength and stability, making it a highly effective exercise for building core endurance and control.

Planche

The planche is one of the most iconic and impressive figures of Street Workout. This static position, where the body is kept parallel to the ground only by the strength of the arms, requires a perfect combination of strength, balance and body control.

Front-lever

The front lever, an advanced calisthenics exercise, involves suspending yourself horizontally beneath a bar or rings. Achieving this position requires pulling your shoulders back, creating a straight, plank-like alignment of your body.

Back-lever

The backlever (hanging scale rearways) is an isometric anterior chain static element. Backlever as portrayed in the MAG COP. When performing a backlever, you hold your body horizontally above the floor, stomach down, arms in the back holding the execution surface.
